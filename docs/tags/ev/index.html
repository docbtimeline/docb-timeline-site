Every Machine in China's Battery Line Is Chinese

Every Machine in China’s Battery Line Is Chinese A procurement manager at Volkswagen’s Wolfsburg headquarters is sourcing cells for the next ID-series platform. The most competitive bids on her desk come from China — and the gap is widening. Not because European batteries don’t exist as a concept, but because every factory that was supposed to make them is now bankrupt, shuttered, or repurposed. 620 Miles and No Factory to Build It In the first quarter of 2025, a Volkswagen prototype fitted with Gotion High Tech’s solid-state battery completed vehicle testing with a range of 1,000 kilometers — 620 miles on a single charge. A solid-state battery replaces the liquid electrolyte in conventional lithium-ion cells with a solid material, eliminating flammability risk and boosting energy density.
